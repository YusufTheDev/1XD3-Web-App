Game Instructions

Welcome to the infinitetravel:

  1. Use a,s,d,f to control it going up, and h j k l going down.
  2. Collect coins to level up your characters.
  3. Avoid obstacles and enemies to stay alive.
  4. Don't touch anything sharp.
  5. Pause the game anytime by pressing the "esc" key.

Good luck and have fun!

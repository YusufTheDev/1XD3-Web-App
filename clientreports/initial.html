Initial Client Report

Initial Report: Web App Requirements

Target Users

The target users of the web app are primarily mobile users, although it will also be accessible on desktop devices for flexibility. The users are expected to be tech-savvy individuals who value a clean and user-friendly interface. Additionally, accessibility features should be considered to ensure the app can be used by individuals with disabilities, such as screen readers or high-contrast modes.

Basic Static Information

The app should provide the following static information:

  • Company Name
  • Company Address
  • Contact Information (Phone Number, Email Address)
  • About Us Section
  • Links to Social Media Profiles

Design Elements

The client desires a modern, minimalist design with a focus on usability and simplicity. Key design elements include:

  • A color palette that reflects the brand identity (e.g., blue and white for a professional feel).
  • Easy-to-read typography, with sans-serif fonts for clarity.
  • Responsive design to ensure the app works seamlessly on both mobile and desktop devices.
  • Clean layouts with adequate white space to avoid visual clutter.

The client does not want:

  • Overly bright or harsh colors.
  • Distracting animations or pop-ups.
  • Excessive text or overcrowded elements on the screen.

Data Management

The client needs the app to manage and present the following data:

  • User profiles and account information.
  • User-generated content, such as reviews or feedback.
  • Leaderboards for gamified elements, if applicable.
  • Schedules or event details.

Interactive Features

The app could benefit from the inclusion of simple interactive elements, such as:

  • A leaderboard or gamified feature for engaging users.
  • Light animations or hover effects to improve user engagement without overwhelming the interface.
